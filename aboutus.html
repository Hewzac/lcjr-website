About Us

- Aiden Mitchell

"When Logan and I created LCJR, all we thought about was efficiency."

LCJR was started on March 30th by two 17-year-olds with a truck, a work ethic, and a simple idea. We wanted to bring a junk removal service to the Lewis-Clark Valley, all while maintaining speed, affordability, and most importantly, reliability. We make sure that every job is treated with the same respect, and our values are straightforward: to show up on time, work hard, be fair, and always leave the site cleaner than we found it.
